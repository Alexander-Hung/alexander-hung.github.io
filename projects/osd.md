## Project Description

One specific effort is related to a resource decision that the Government of Indonesia is trying to make in coordination with the United States. The Indonesians are determining if a specific portion of land in the northern part of the country could be developed to support commercial activity, military
operations, or a combination of commercial and military activities. They want to understand what the ramifications are from both an economic and military angle, along with possible political considerations. OUSD(P) has determined that a decision model would be the best way to provide the Government of Indonesia all of the considerations, which would enable them to make the best decision in their
interests. They are looking for a team to design this model using Commercial Off the Shelf software, and able to be used on Indonesian systems.
